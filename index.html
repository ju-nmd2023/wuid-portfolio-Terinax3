    Hi, I'm Tereza

    About Me

    My passion lies in the world of aesthetic. As I graduated high school as a Fashion Designer I continued my studies in Serbia, educating myself in Graphic Design.On one hand, I'm deeply in love with art, illustration, drawing, and painting and on the other, I deeply appreciate technology.

    In my teen era, I immersed myself in video games but also into photography and design, sharing my explorations through a blog. It was in this creative space that I first encountered possibilities of web design. With my limited knowledge of HTML and CSS, I tinkered with my blog, striving to transform it into a captivating digital gallery.

    Technology also played a significant role in bringing my one true love into my life, ultimately leading me to Sweden. It is here, in the heart of a country renowned for its excellence in design, that I found the perfect opportunity to learn and master the art of design.

    My Projects

    In past year I have been working on developing my skills in HTML & CSS through help of my brother and websites like W3Schools and Stack Overflow. The majority of my work is born from a genuine desire to learn, and while it may be amateur in nature, it reflects my heartfelt dedication to self-improvement.
