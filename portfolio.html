    Imagidice

    As a starting project my brother and I set ourselves a challenging hobby project to create a web-based version of a boardgame called Imagidice. Essentially it's a storytelling game, with players rolling the six-sided dice, then using the images on them to create a story one die at a time. I have been working on front-end, meanwhile he was coding the back-end of the website. While this project is still a work in progress, it's something I take great pride in.

    Scholomance

    One of my other projects involved the creation of a website for a school or academy. In a whimsical twist, I decided to craft a website for a school called Scholomance, a fictitious institution of necromancy, a dungeon instance of the popular video game, World of Warcraft.

    Tasty Byte

    As I left my homecountry and moved to Sweden I have tried many new delicious dishes and shared some of my own too, but photographing recipes or sending links to my family and friends wasn't an option because of the language barrier. Therefore to bridge the language gap and share recipes effectively, I decided to create a website featuring recipes in Serbian, English, and Swedish.

    Whorl

    My most recent project involved the creation of an e-commerce website. For a long time I wished to start such project but I hesitated due to my limited experience in JavaScript. I posponed until my curiosity overcame my apprehension.

    Interestingly, my inspiration for starting this project came from the last item I considered buying. When I discovered Loop Earplugs, it made me realize the importance of even small and seemingly insignificant items in people's lives. This reinforced my belief that quality products, regardless of their size, can be presented in a captivating and meaningful way.
